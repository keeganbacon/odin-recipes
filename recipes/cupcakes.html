Cupcake recipe

A classic cupcake that's sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans

steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes.
  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  5. Gradually stir in the dry ingredients, alternating with the milk, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined.
  6. Stir in the cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger.
  7. Drop tablespoonfuls of the dough onto the prepared muffin tin, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  8. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until the cupcakes are golden brown.
  9. Let cool completely on the muffin tin before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

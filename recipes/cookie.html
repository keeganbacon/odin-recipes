Chocolate chip cookie recipe

A classic cookie that's sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 cups brown sugar, packed
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter and white sugar until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. In a separate mixing bowl, sift together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually stir the dry ingredients into the butter mixture, alternating with the milk, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are set and the centers are still slightly jiggly.
  9. Let cool completely on the baking sheet before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
